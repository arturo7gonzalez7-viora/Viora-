# Casa Mariachi Brand Guidelines

## Brand Colors
**Primary Colors (use most often):**
- Orange: #fca62d
- Red: #fc1505
- Yellow: #ffce00
- Black: #000000

**Secondary/Accent:**
- Dark Green: #064e09
- Orange Accent: #fd6003
- Bright Green: #13b419

## Typography
- **Title Font:** Montserrat Bold (Reel hooks, promo headlines)
- **Subtitle Font:** Montserrat Regular (supporting text, dates)
- **Heading Font:** Oswald Bold (prices, dollar signs, punch words)
- **Body Font:** Poppins Regular (descriptions, menu text)

## Design Style
- Bright, warm, clean
- Natural lighting preferred
- Avoid cold blue/gray filters
- No neon or pastel colors
- Text must have strong contrast
- Always professional and welcoming

## Gift Card Design Requirements
Based on references:
- Clean geometric Mexican border patterns
- Cream/beige center background
- Logo prominently displayed center
- "GIFT CARD" text using brand typography
- Warm brand colors in borders/patterns
- Professional restaurant quality
