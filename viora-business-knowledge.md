## Business Summary

**Viora (Avian Agency LLC)** — AI voice agent service helping service-based businesses never miss calls and book more appointments.

### Core Offering
- AI voice agents answer calls 24/7
- Qualify leads naturally (one question at a time)
- Book appointments directly into Google Calendar
- Log all call data in Google Sheets

### Target Market
- Home service companies
- Real estate professionals
- Home repair contractors
- High call-volume service businesses

### Tech Stack
- **Retell AI** — voice agent infrastructure
- **n8n** — automation workflows
- **Google Calendar** — booking system
- **Google Sheets** — data logging
- **Typeform** — lead capture forms
- **Google Meet** — sales calls

### Pricing
- **Setup:** $2,500 (50% upfront, 50% after completion)
- **Monthly recurring:** varies by client
- **Ad-based offer (specific cases):** $1k start, $3k/mo after

### Outreach Infrastructure
- **Domain:** viora-co.com (Google Workspace)
- **Methods:** Cold email, LinkedIn (free account)
- **VA support:** Email/LinkedIn outreach, follow-ups, data logging

### Sales Process
1. Cold outreach (email/LinkedIn)
2. Book strategy call (Google Calendar)
3. Sales call (Google Meet)
4. Close client
5. Collect payment (50% upfront)
6. Build AI system
7. Test
8. Deploy
9. Collect remaining 50%

### Core Operating Principles
- AI must sound natural
- Ask one question at a time
- No unnecessary closing statements
- Maintain human-like flow
- Keep calls smooth and professional

### Business Goal
Scale Viora by:
- Increasing outreach volume
- Booking more demos
- Closing more service-based clients
- Building recurring revenue base
- Target: $20k/mo revenue, onboard clients weekly
