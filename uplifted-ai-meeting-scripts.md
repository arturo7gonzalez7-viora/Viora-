# UPLIFTED AI MEETING SCRIPTS
## Word-for-Word Talking Points

---

## OPENING (2-3 minutes)

**Pre-Meeting Setup:**
"Thanks for taking the time to meet with me today. I know your time is valuable, so I'm going to keep this focused and show you exactly how we helped Daniel get an $80,000 qualified lead in his first week working with us."

**Permission to Present:**
"I've got about 15 minutes of content to share with you, and then I'd love to answer any questions you have. Does that work for your schedule today?"

**Credibility Builder:**
"Before we dive in, Daniel specifically asked me to meet with you because he knows how hard it is to get consistent, quality leads in the home service business. He's already seen what this system can do, and he thought you'd want to see it too."

---

## PROBLEM IDENTIFICATION (Slide 1)

**The Pain Point Hook:**
"Let me ask you something - show of hands, how many phone calls did you miss last week while you were on a job site?" 

[Wait for response]

**Amplify the Pain:**
"Here's what I've learned working with guys like Daniel - every single missed call is costing you between $500 and $2,000 in lost revenue. And the bigger problem? While you're focused on doing great work for your current customers, your competitors are booking your next customers."

**The Real Problem:**
"Most home service businesses lose 30-40% of their leads to competitors simply because they can't respond fast enough. You're not losing because your work isn't good enough - you're losing because you can't be in two places at once."

**Statistical Impact:**
"If you're missing even 3 calls a week, that's potentially $6,000 in lost revenue monthly. Over a year, that's $72,000 walking out the door. Does that sound about right based on your experience?"

---

## SOLUTION PRESENTATION (Slide 2)

**The Transition:**
"So what if there was a way to capture every single prospect while you're focused on the work you love doing?"

**System Overview:**
"Here's what we built - we call it the Never-Miss-A-Customer System. It handles your entire customer acquisition process from the first time someone sees your ad to the moment they're sitting in your driveway ready to sign a contract."

**The Flow Explanation:**
"Here's how it works: We create professional video ads that make people stop scrolling and think 'I need to call these guys.' When they respond, our AI immediately starts a conversation with them - while you're working. It qualifies their budget, timeline, and project scope. Then it books qualified appointments directly into your calendar."

**The Key Differentiator:**
"Most companies sell you leads - basically phone numbers of people who might be interested. We sell you customers - people who are pre-qualified, pre-interested, and pre-booked into your calendar."

**Real-World Example:**
"So instead of you chasing down 20 leads to find 2 good ones, we send you 2 people who are already ready to buy. You spend your time doing estimates for people who actually want to hire you, not tire-kickers."

---

## PROOF SECTION (Slide 3)

**Transition:**
"I know this probably sounds too good to be true. Six months ago, Daniel said the exact same thing. Let me show you exactly what happened..."

**Daniel's Story Setup:**
"Daniel runs a home remodeling business just like yours. He was skeptical about AI, skeptical about ads, skeptical about the whole thing. But he was tired of the feast-or-famine cycle, so he decided to give it a shot."

**Week 1 Results:**
"In his first week - literally day 6 after we went live - he got a call from a guy looking to do an $80,000 full home renovation. This wasn't a tire-kicker - this was a qualified prospect who'd already been through our AI qualification process."

**30-Day Results:**
"By the end of his first 30 days, Daniel had 23 qualified phone conversations with people interested in his services. Not leads that went nowhere - actual conversations with people ready to move forward."

**Quality Focus:**
"Here's the key - every single project that came through was $5,000 or more. We weren't sending him people looking to fix a leaky faucet. We were sending him the projects that make his business profitable."

**Time Savings:**
"And Daniel told me the best part wasn't even the leads - it was getting his evenings back. No more spending 3 hours a night calling people who don't answer or aren't serious."

**Credibility Close:**
"Daniel was so impressed that he specifically asked me to meet with you and [other prospect]. That's how confident he is that this will work for your business too."

---

## OFFER PRESENTATION (Slide 4)

**Value Stack Introduction:**
"Alright, so you've seen the problem, you've seen the solution, and you've seen the proof. Let me show you exactly what you get and what it's worth."

**Value Breakdown:**
"If you were to build this system yourself, here's what you'd need:

First, you'd need professional advertising - a good agency charges $2,500 a month just for ad management.

Then you'd need a content creation team to make those scroll-stopping videos - that's another $1,500 a month.

You'd need an AI automation system that can have intelligent conversations with prospects - companies like that charge $2,000 a month.

You'd need a CRM system to track all your leads - that's $1,000 a month.

And you'd need someone managing all of this for you - a success manager runs $1,000 a month.

That's $8,000 a month in value."

**The Investment:**
"Now, I could charge you $8,000 a month and it would be worth every penny based on Daniel's results. But here's what we're going to do instead."

**The Offer:**
"Your total investment to get started is $1,700. That covers everything - the complete system setup, the initial ad spend, everything you need to start generating qualified leads in the next 10 days."

**The Guarantee:**
"And here's how confident I am that this will work for you - we guarantee you'll get at least 15 qualified phone conversations in your first 30 days, or we'll keep running your ads for free until you do. You literally cannot lose."

**Timeline:**
"From the moment you say yes today, we'll have your complete system live and generating leads within 10 days. Not 30 days, not 60 days - 10 days."

---

## OBJECTION HANDLING

### "That's a lot of money upfront"

"I get it, $1,700 feels like a big investment. Let me put it in perspective for you. Daniel's first lead was worth $80,000. Even if he closes that at a 30% margin, that's $24,000 profit from one lead. The system pays for itself 14 times over with just that one customer. And remember, he got 22 more qualified conversations on top of that."

### "How do I know this will work for my business?"

"That's exactly what Daniel asked me. Here's what I told him - we only work with home service businesses in Colorado. Your market, your customers, your competition. Daniel's in home remodeling, you're in [specific trade]. The homeowners are the same people with the same problems. If it works for Daniel, it'll work for you."

### "I need to think about it"

"I totally understand - this is a business decision. Let me ask you this - what specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the timeline, or something else? Because if I can answer those questions right now, we can get you started this week instead of waiting."

### "I want to talk to my partner/wife first"

"Absolutely, that makes sense. Most successful business owners I work with make decisions together. What questions do you think they'll have? Let's make sure you have all the answers you need for that conversation."

---

## CLOSING SEQUENCE (Slide 5)

**Scarcity Setup:**
"Here's the thing - and I need to be completely transparent with you about this - we only work with 3 companies per month. Our content creation team can't handle more than that and still deliver the quality you deserve."

**Social Proof Close:**
"Daniel took spot number one. He's the guy getting the $80,000 lead. I've got two spots left this month."

**Choice Close:**
"So I need to ask you - do you want spot number two, or do you want to wait and see if spot number three is still available next week?"

**Assumptive Close:**
"Based on everything we've talked about today, and seeing what Daniel accomplished in his first month, does this make sense for your business?"

**Direct Close:**
"Are you ready to guarantee you never miss another customer again?"

---

## AFTER THE YES

**Immediate Next Steps:**
"Fantastic! Here's what happens next. I'm going to have you sign this service agreement, and then we'll schedule your kickoff call for tomorrow morning. My team will need about 30 minutes with you to get all the details about your business, your ideal customers, and your service area."

**Timeline Confirmation:**
"We'll have your ads live and generating leads by [specific date - 7-10 days]. You should start seeing qualified prospects calling you within 48 hours of launch."

**Expectation Setting:**
"In the first week, you'll probably get 3-5 qualified conversations. By week 3, that should be up to 5-7 per week. Remember, we're measuring quality, not quantity."

---

## BACKUP CLOSES IF THEY'RE HESITANT

### The Daniel Reference Close:
"Look, I get that this is a big decision. Would it help if you talked to Daniel directly? I can give you his number right now and you can ask him anything you want about working with us."

### The Risk Reversal Close:
"What if we did this - what if I guarantee that if you're not completely satisfied with the quality of leads we're sending you in the first 30 days, I'll give you every penny back and you keep the entire system? Does that remove the risk for you?"

### The Competitor Close:
"I need to ask you something - if your biggest competitor had this system and you didn't, how would that affect your business? Because I guarantee someone in your market is going to say yes to something like this. The question is whether it's you or them."

---

*Remember: This is a conversation, not a monologue. Pause for questions, get agreement, and read their body language throughout.*
