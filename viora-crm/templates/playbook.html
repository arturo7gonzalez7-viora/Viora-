🤖 Automation Cheat Sheet

Everything you can build, how to build it, and what to charge.

1. AI Receptionist (Voice)

$350/mo Build time: 1-2 days Easy

What it does: Answers every phone call 24/7. Qualifies the lead. Books appointments directly into their Google Calendar. Speaks English and Spanish.

Build with: Retell AI + Google Calendar API

ROI pitch: "Missing 5 calls/week at $500 avg job = $10,000/month lost. This fixes it for $350/month."

Best for: HVAC, roofing, med spas, law firms, salons, gyms, anyone who gets phone calls

How to build:

  • Create agent in Retell AI dashboard
  • Train it on their business (services, FAQs, pricing)
  • Connect Google Calendar for booking
  • Forward their business phone to Retell number
  • Test 20+ call scenarios before launch

2. Missed Call Text-Back

$100/mo addon Build time: 2 hours Easy

What it does: When someone calls and nobody answers, they get an automated text within 60 seconds: "Hey! We just missed your call. How can we help?"

Build with: n8n Cloud + Twilio + their phone system webhook

ROI pitch: "78% of customers buy from whoever responds first. You respond in 60 seconds automatically."

Best for: Any business that misses calls occasionally

How to build:

  • Set up Twilio phone number or use their existing number
  • Create n8n webhook that fires on missed call event
  • Connect to Twilio SMS node
  • Customize message with their business name
  • Test by calling and not answering

3. Review Request Automation

$150/mo Build time: 3 hours Easy

What it does: After every completed job or appointment, customer automatically gets a text asking for a Google review with a direct link.

Build with: n8n Cloud + Twilio + their scheduling/CRM system

ROI pitch: "1 star increase on Google = 5-9% more revenue. Businesses using automated review requests get 3x more reviews in 60 days."

Best for: Local service businesses, salons, HVAC, cleaning companies, contractors

How to build:

  • Connect to their job completion trigger (Jobber, ServiceTitan, or manual webhook)
  • Build n8n workflow: job complete → wait 2 hours → send SMS
  • Message: "Thanks for choosing [Business]! Would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? [direct link]"
  • Get their Google review direct link from Google Business Profile

4. Follow-Up Sequence Automation

$200/mo Build time: 4 hours Medium

What it does: When someone fills out a form, calls but doesn't book, or requests a quote, they automatically get a 3-touch follow-up sequence over 7 days via SMS and email.

Build with: n8n Cloud + Twilio + Gmail API

ROI pitch: "55% of sales happen after follow-up 5+. Most businesses follow up once. Automating a 3-touch sequence recovers 20-30% of lost leads."

Best for: Contractors, roofing, HVAC, law firms, anyone who gives quotes

How to build:

  • Trigger: new form submission or new lead in their CRM
  • Day 1: Immediate text "Hey [name], thanks for reaching out to [business]! I will follow up with you shortly."
  • Day 3: Email with case study or relevant proof
  • Day 7: Final follow-up text with offer or question
  • Stop sequence if they reply or book

5. Appointment Reminder System

$100/mo addon Build time: 2 hours Easy

What it does: Automatically texts clients 24 hours and 2 hours before their appointment. Reduces no-shows dramatically.

Build with: n8n Cloud + Twilio + Google Calendar

ROI pitch: "Reduces no-shows by 29%. A salon with 100 appointments/month at $80 avg saves $2,300/month in lost revenue."

Best for: Salons, med spas, gyms, chiropractors, any appointment-based business

How to build:

  • Connect n8n to their Google Calendar
  • Trigger: new calendar event created
  • Schedule two texts: 24 hours before and 2 hours before
  • Message: "Reminder: Your appointment at [Business] is tomorrow at [time]. Reply CONFIRM or CANCEL."
  • If CANCEL: notify owner immediately

6. Reactivation Campaign

$150/mo Build time: 3 hours Easy

What it does: Automatically reaches out to past customers who haven't returned in 60-90 days with a personal message and offer to bring them back.

Build with: n8n Cloud + Twilio + their CRM or Google Sheets

ROI pitch: "Getting a past customer back costs 5x less than finding a new one. Most businesses have 200+ past customers sitting ignored in their database."

Best for: Salons, gyms, cleaning services, any business with repeat customers

How to build:

  • Pull customer list from their CRM or Google Sheets
  • Filter: last visit more than 60 days ago
  • Monthly automated text: "Hey [name], we miss you at [Business]! It has been a while. Here is 10% off your next visit: [booking link]"

📅 How to Run the Business Daily

The rule: Do these 3 things every single day. No exceptions. The business grows when you are consistent. It stalls when you skip days.
1

Morning — Open CRM, Send Audit Pitches (20 min)

Open the CRM TODAY tab. Send 40 LinkedIn DMs using the audit pitch from the queue. Copy, paste, send. That is it. 40 per day = 200 per week = 2 to 5 demos booked.

2

Anytime — Respond to Replies Within 1 Hour

Speed wins deals. When someone replies on LinkedIn or email, respond immediately. Book the audit call while they are still warm. The CRM Hot Leads card shows you who needs a response.

3

Daily — Post One Piece of Content on LinkedIn (5 min)

One post per day. Use these formats rotating through the week: Monday: case study or result, Wednesday: industry stat + what it means, Friday: behind the scenes of what you build. Content creates inbound leads that close at 50%+ because they came to you.

📞 The Audit Call Script

When someone books an audit, run this call. 20 minutes max.

OPENER (30 seconds): "Hey [name], appreciate you jumping on. Quick agenda — I will ask you two or three questions about your business, then I will show you exactly what I would automate and what the ROI looks like. Sound good?" DISCOVERY (3-5 minutes, ask these): 1. "What is the number one thing eating your team's time every week?" 2. "How many leads or calls do you think you lose every week that you never get back to?" 3. "If you could automate just one thing in your business this month, what would make the biggest difference?" SHOW THE AUDIT (5 minutes): Pull up the AI Audit report you ran on their website. "Based on what you just told me and what I found on your site, here are the top two things I would automate for you..." Walk through the ROI for each. THE CLOSE: "Here is what I can build for you in the next two weeks. The pilot is $750. I build it, we run it for 30 days, I show you the results. If you are happy we make it permanent at $[X]/month. If not, that is it, no pressure. Want to move forward?" THEN STOP TALKING. Wait for their answer. IF YES: "Perfect. I am sending you a payment link right now." Send Stripe link. Stay on the call until it is paid. IF HESITATING: "What specifically would you need to feel good about moving forward?" IF NO: "No worries at all. If AI automation ever becomes something worth exploring, we are at viora-co.com."

🤝 How to Handle Client CRMs

Key point: You do not replace their existing software. You plug AI into it.

  • GoHighLevel: n8n has native integration. Easy.
  • HubSpot: n8n has native integration. Easy.
  • Jobber / ServiceTitan / Housecall Pro: Webhooks available. Medium.
  • Google Sheets / Calendly / Acuity: All native n8n integrations. Easy.
  • Squire (barbershops): No public API. Workaround: missed call text-back, review automation, and follow-up sequences all still work without a direct integration.
  • Unknown / custom software: Ask "Does it have webhooks or an API?" If yes, you can connect it.

Sales line: "We do not ask you to change anything. We plug AI into your existing setup and make it smarter."

🚨 Rules to Never Break

  • Never send cold email from your main domain. Use a separate sending domain only.
  • Never send to generic emails (info@, contact@). Owner emails only.
  • Never overpromise on delivery time. Say 14 days, deliver in 14 days.
  • Never skip a day of LinkedIn DMs. Consistency is everything.
  • Always collect payment before starting the build. 50% upfront minimum.
  • Always follow up within 1 hour of a reply. Speed wins.
  • Always show results at day 30. Numbers keep clients. Stories do not.
