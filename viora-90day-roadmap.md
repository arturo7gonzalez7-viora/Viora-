# Viora 90-Day Growth Roadmap to $20k/mo

**Goal:** Scale Viora to $20,000/month recurring revenue in 90 days

**Math:**
- Average monthly recurring: $500-750/client
- Clients needed: 27-40 active monthly clients
- Setup fees during ramp: $2,500 × 6-8 clients/mo = $15k-20k one-time revenue/mo

**Strategy:** Close 6-8 new clients per month consistently while building systems that scale.

---

## MONTH 1 (Weeks 1-4): Foundation & Systems

### Week 1: Outreach Infrastructure
**Goal:** Scale cold outreach to 100+ contacts/day

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Audit current email deliverability (viora-co.com domain health)
- [ ] Confirm SPF, DKIM, DMARC records are correct
- [ ] Warm up 2-3 new email accounts (send 10-20/day, increase daily)
- [ ] Build targeted prospect lists (500+ contacts):
  - Home service companies (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, landscaping)
  - Real estate agents (high call volume)
  - Contractors (electrical, remodeling, painting)
- [ ] Set up LinkedIn automation (free account, manual 20-30 DMs/day)
- [ ] Train VA on new templates and follow-up cadence

**Metrics:**
- 100 cold emails/day
- 20 LinkedIn DMs/day
- Target: 15-20 replies/week

---

### Week 2: Sales Process Optimization
**Goal:** Tighten the sales call and close rate

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Record 3-5 sales calls (Google Meet recordings)
- [ ] Analyze objections and common questions
- [ ] Refine qualification script (use template #2)
- [ ] Create a "demo video" (3-5 min Loom showing AI in action)
- [ ] Build a one-page case study or testimonial (if you have one)
- [ ] Practice pricing presentation (template #3) — aim for <30 sec delivery

**Metrics:**
- Book 10+ strategy calls this week
- Close 2-3 clients
- Track close rate (aim for 25-30%)

---

### Week 3: Delivery & Automation
**Goal:** Speed up client onboarding and deployment

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Document the full build process (step-by-step checklist)
- [ ] Create Retell AI agent templates (reusable for future clients)
- [ ] Build n8n automation templates (call logging, calendar booking)
- [ ] Set up a standardized Google Sheets template for clients
- [ ] Reduce time-to-deployment: target 5-7 days from payment to go-live
- [ ] Automate client onboarding email sequence (template #6)

**Metrics:**
- Onboard 2-3 new clients this week
- Deployment time: <7 days
- Zero missed setup deadlines

---

### Week 4: Content & Credibility
**Goal:** Build trust signals and inbound lead sources

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Post 3-5 LinkedIn updates (client wins, before/after stats)
- [ ] Create a simple case study PDF (show results: "X missed calls → 0 missed calls")
- [ ] Record a "day in the life" video (building an AI agent, client call, deployment)
- [ ] Update viora-co.com homepage (add testimonial or case study)
- [ ] Start a weekly LinkedIn newsletter (AI for service businesses)
- [ ] Engage with 10-20 target prospects daily (comment, like, share)

**Metrics:**
- 3-5 inbound leads from LinkedIn activity
- 1-2 inbound demo requests from website

---

### MONTH 1 TARGET: $7,500-12,500 in setup fees (3-5 clients closed)

## MONTH 2 (Weeks 5-8): Scale & Systematize

### Week 5: Outreach Volume Increase
**Goal:** Double outreach capacity

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Add 2 more email accounts (5 total sending accounts)
- [ ] Increase daily outreach to 200 emails/day
- [ ] LinkedIn: 30 DMs/day
- [ ] Build lookalike lists (target similar businesses to closed clients)
- [ ] A/B test 2-3 new email subject lines
- [ ] Track reply rates by industry (home service vs real estate vs contractors)

**Metrics:**
- 200 emails/day
- 30 LinkedIn DMs/day
- Target: 25-30 replies/week

---

### Week 6: Referral System
**Goal:** Turn happy clients into lead sources

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Email all active clients: "If you know anyone who needs this, we'll give you $500 off your next month"
- [ ] Create a simple referral one-pager (PDF or webpage)
- [ ] Ask for Google reviews or testimonials
- [ ] Offer a "free month" for every successful referral
- [ ] Follow up with 3 past clients (check-in calls)

**Metrics:**
- 1-2 referral leads this week
- 1-2 new testimonials collected

---

### Week 7: Paid Advertising Test
**Goal:** Test paid lead gen (Google Ads or Facebook Ads)

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Set up Google Ads campaign: "AI Receptionist for [Home Services/Real Estate]"
- [ ] Budget: $500-1000/week (test)
- [ ] Landing page: simple Typeform or Calendly link
- [ ] Track cost per booked call
- [ ] Optimize ad copy based on click-through rate
- [ ] Retarget website visitors (if traffic exists)

**Metrics:**
- 5-10 booked strategy calls from ads
- Cost per booked call: <$50
- Close 1-2 clients from paid traffic

---

### Week 8: Upsell & Retention
**Goal:** Increase revenue per client

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Audit existing clients: who could use outbound calling?
- [ ] Offer add-on services (e.g., Typeform → AI outbound follow-up)
- [ ] Create a "premium plan" (higher monthly fee for advanced features)
- [ ] Check in with clients: track satisfaction and identify churn risk
- [ ] Document win stories (before/after call volume, booked appointments)

**Metrics:**
- 1-2 clients upgrade to higher monthly plan
- Zero churn (all clients retained)

---

### MONTH 2 TARGET: $15,000-20,000 in setup fees (6-8 clients closed)

## MONTH 3 (Weeks 9-12): Momentum & Scale

### Week 9: Partnerships & Alliances
**Goal:** Build referral partnerships

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Reach out to 10 complementary service providers:
  - Marketing agencies (who serve home service businesses)
  - Web designers (who build sites for contractors)
  - CRM consultants (who work with real estate agents)
- [ ] Offer 20% commission on every referral
- [ ] Create a simple partner one-sheet (what you do, who you serve, commission structure)
- [ ] Schedule 5+ partner intro calls

**Metrics:**
- 2-3 active referral partners
- 1-2 leads from partners this month

---

### Week 10: Automation Overhaul
**Goal:** Remove yourself from repetitive tasks

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Automate follow-up sequences (email drip campaigns in n8n or Make)
- [ ] Use Calendly or similar for auto-booking strategy calls (no back-and-forth)
- [ ] Create a self-serve demo (Loom video + FAQ page)
- [ ] Build a Notion or Airtable CRM (track all prospects and pipeline)
- [ ] Delegate VA tasks: list building, data entry, follow-ups

**Metrics:**
- Save 5-10 hours/week on admin tasks
- VA handles 80% of outreach follow-up

---

### Week 11: Case Studies & Social Proof
**Goal:** Document wins and leverage them

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Create 3 detailed case studies (before/after, client testimonials, metrics)
- [ ] Post weekly LinkedIn content (client stories, tips, insights)
- [ ] Start a YouTube channel or podcast (optional, long-term)
- [ ] Update website with fresh case studies
- [ ] Share wins in cold emails and sales calls

**Metrics:**
- 3 published case studies
- 5-10% increase in reply rates due to social proof

---

### Week 12: Reflect & Optimize
**Goal:** Review the 90 days and plan next quarter

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Calculate total revenue (setup fees + monthly recurring)
- [ ] Review close rate, reply rate, cost per acquisition
- [ ] Identify bottlenecks (outreach, sales, delivery)
- [ ] Hire or delegate weak points (e.g., hire SDR for outreach)
- [ ] Set new goals for Q2 (e.g., $40k/mo by end of Q2)
- [ ] Celebrate wins and document lessons learned

**Metrics:**
- Total clients: 15-20 active monthly clients
- Monthly recurring revenue: $7,500-15,000
- Setup fees (Q1 total): $37,500-50,000
- Path to $20k/mo: clear and achievable in next 30 days
