## Reel Editing Standards (2025-2026)

**Cut Pacing:** 1-2 second cuts max. Never let a shot breathe longer than needed. Movement = retention.

**Beat Sync:** Cut on major beats. Every edit follows the rhythm of the music. Hook viewers in the first second.

**Speed Ramping:** Slow down pours/reveals, speed up repetitive prep. Shoot at 48fps+ for fluid ramps. Never overdo it.

**Trending Transitions:** Ingredient reveal, swipe plate, smoke/steam reveal. Directional flow between cuts.

**Color Grading:** Warm tones always. Do speed edits first, then apply color. Boost saturation + contrast for food.

**Key Shots to Always Capture:**
- Overhead: ingredients laid out, plating
- Side angle: sauce pours, steam rising
- Macro/close-up: cheese pulls, crispy textures, garnish drops, drink pours

**Text Overlays:** Clean, minimal. Dish name, ingredients, or price. Never clutter.

**Captions:** Always. Essential for silent scrollers.

**Hook Rule:** First frame = most visually striking shot. Cheese pull, sizzle, pour. NEVER open with setup.
