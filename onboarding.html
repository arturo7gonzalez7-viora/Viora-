    Quick FAQ


    Q: What if the AI gets something wrong?

    A: It happens rarely, but when it does, we fix it fast. Call transcripts let us see exactly what happened and update the AI immediately. Most issues are resolved within 24 hours.


    Q: Can I still answer calls myself?

    A: Absolutely. The AI answers when you can't (after hours, busy, etc.). You can always pick up the phone yourself — the AI only kicks in if you don't answer.


    Q: Will my customers know it's AI?

    A: Modern AI voice agents sound incredibly natural. Most callers won't notice, and those who do are usually impressed. The AI identifies itself as a "virtual assistant" if asked directly.


    Q: What if I want to change something?

    A: Just let us know! Want to update your hours? Add a new service? Change how the AI greets people? We make changes within 24 hours.
