# Alex Hormozi Business Frameworks - Quick Reference Cheat Sheet

## CORE PHILOSOPHY
- **Revenue is vanity. Profit is sanity. Cash is reality.**
- Get Flow → Monetize Flow → Add Friction
- "If you got hit by a bus today, what would keep the business growing tomorrow?"

---

## 1. YOUR FIRST AVATAR (Finding High-Value Customers)

**The Vista Method** - 4 Steps:
1. **Survey customers**: Demographics, business stats, aspirations, buying process
2. **Find biggest spenders**: Top 20% who spent most + stayed longest
3. **Find commonalities**: 3-5 qualifiers they all share
4. **Execute**: Speak to that avatar, re-engineer sales process

**Result**: Replace low-value customers with high-value ones = 5x growth (Pareto on steroids)

---

## 2. FAST CASH PLAYS

**When to Use**: Need cash quickly (taxes, expenses, growth capital)
**How Often**: Every 90 days recommended

**Two Main Sequences**:

### Push to Consult
- High-ticket offer ($3,000-$10,000+)
- Personal consultation/strategy session
- Limited spots (creates urgency)

### Push to Automated Checkout
- Premium offer ($500-$2,000)
- Automated purchase flow
- Time-limited promotion

**Structure**: 7-day email/text sequence
**Expected Results**: 5-10 buyers at $5,000+ per promotion

---

## 3. HOOKS (Double Marketing in <5 Seconds)

**Key Principle**: "After you've written your headline, you've spent eighty cents of your advertising dollar" - David Ogilvy

**Types**:
- **Verbal**: First 5 seconds of copy/video
- **Nonverbal**: Visual pattern interrupts

**Best Performing Hook Patterns**:
- "How I [achieved result] without [common struggle]"
- "[Number] ways to [desired outcome]"
- "The [unexpected thing] that [created result]"
- Story hooks (personal transformation)

---

## 4. LIFETIME VALUE - THE CRAZY 8

**8 Ways to Increase Customer Value**:

1. **Increase Prices** - Raise what you charge
2. **Decrease Costs** - Lower what it costs to deliver
3. **Increase # of Purchases** - Get them to buy more often
4. **Cross-Sell** - Sell something different
5. **Sell More** - Increase quantity per purchase
6. **Sell Better** - Upgrade to higher quality
7. **Downsell Fewer** - Offer lower quantity option (retain customer)
8. **Downsell Lower Quality** - Offer budget option (retain customer)

**Formula**: LTV = (Average Purchase Value × # of Purchases) - Cost to Serve

---

## 5. MARKETING MACHINE (Endless Ads Without You)

**5 Content Sources**:

1. **Lifecycle Ads** - Document customer journey stages
2. **Social Media Scrape** - Turn customer posts into ads
3. **In-Person Events** - Record testimonials and experiences
4. **Community Scrape** - Mine Slack/Discord/forums for wins
5. **Competitions & Awards** - Document achievements

**Goal**: Business grows without your face in every ad

---

## 6. PRICE RAISE FRAMEWORK (RAISE Method)

**R** - Remind them of value provided already
**A** - Address the price change directly
**I** - Invest in their future (what's coming)
**S** - Soften with loyalty reward (grandfather existing customers)
**E** - Explain away concerns (FAQs)

**Rules**:
- Raise prices annually minimum
- Grandfather existing customers for 30-90 days
- Add value before/during raise
- Small percentages = big changes (10-20% typical)

---

## 7. PRICING STRATEGIES

**Three Main Models**:
1. **Cost-Plus** - Cost + desired margin
2. **Value-Based** - Price based on customer outcome value
3. **Market-Based** - Price based on competition

**Instant Profit Plays**:
- **28-Day Billing** - Bill every 28 days instead of monthly = 13 payments/year vs 12
- **Processing Fees** - Pass on 3% credit card fees to customer
- **Second Form of Payment** - Backup payment method = fewer failed charges

**Key Rule**: "If you have to have a prayer session before raising price by a tenth of a cent, you've got a terrible business" - Warren Buffett

---

## 8. LEAD NURTURE (Get More to Show)

**Goal**: Increase response → schedule → show rates

**Key Tactics**:
- Multi-channel follow-up (email, SMS, phone)
- Value-first nurture sequences
- Address objections before sales call
- Reminders and confirmation sequences
- Social proof in nurture

---

## 9. RETENTION (The 5 Horsemen)

**5 Horsemen of Retention**:
1. **Set Expectations** - Properly frame what's coming
2. **Consistent Delivery** - Do what you said you'd do
3. **Early Wins** - Get results in first 7-30 days
4. **Community** - Create belonging and identity
5. **Ongoing Value** - Continually add new benefits

**Churn Impact**: 15% monthly churn = lose 83% of customers per year
**Goal**: Sub-5% monthly churn for healthy business

---

## 10. FIVE FRAMEWORKS (From 5-Framework PDF)

### 5-Star Service Framework
- Sets delivery standards as you scale
- Goal: Delivery gets BETTER, not worse
- Target: NPS 88+ (vs Apple 61, Chick-fil-A 58)

### Gametape Review Framework
- Weekly cross-functional call review
- Sales + Customer Success watch calls together
- Creates seamless customer experience

### High Performance Communication Framework
- Focus: Energy, Engagement, Exploration
- Prevents meeting bloat
- Increases work quality + quantity per person

### Monday Hour One Framework
- Weekly calendar planning
- Full team transparency
- Manager can see: what everyone's doing, utilization, who needs help, alignment

### Pay Increase Framework
- Clear expectations for raises
- Documents what's rewarded
- How to ask properly
- Frequency and percentage guidelines

## IMPLEMENTATION PRIORITY

**First 90 Days**:
1. Define your avatar (Vista Method)
2. Run a Fast Cash play
3. Implement 28-day billing + processing fees
4. Raise prices 10-15%
5. Start Marketing Machine (pick 2 sources)

**Next 90 Days**:
6. Add 2-3 Crazy 8 tactics to increase LTV
7. Deploy retention framework (5 Horsemen)
8. Test new hooks (aim for 2x click-through rate)
9. Run second Fast Cash play
10. Implement team frameworks (Monday Hour One, 5-Star Service)

## KEY METRICS TO TRACK

- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio** (Target: 3:1 minimum)
- **Monthly Churn %** (Target: <5%)
- **Show Rate** (Target: 60-80%)
- **Close Rate** (Target: 20-40%+)
- **NPS** (Net Promoter Score) (Target: 70+)
