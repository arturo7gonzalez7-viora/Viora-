# Lustere Cleaning Co. — Brand Package Summary

## Research Findings: What Top Colorado Cleaning Companies Do to Win

1. **Trust signals are non-negotiable** — The top Denver cleaning companies (Mop Stars, Reliant Cleaning, etc.) all prominently display "Fully Insured & Bonded," background-checked teams, and satisfaction guarantees above the fold.

2. **Google Business Profile is the #1 local lead source** — Companies that dominate the Denver market have optimized GBP listings with 100+ reviews, photos, and "Free Estimate" CTAs directly in search results.

3. **Recurring service plans drive lifetime value** — Successful companies push weekly/bi-weekly/monthly plans hard. Top companies manage 100+ recurring clients generating predictable monthly revenue.

4. **Eco-friendly positioning is a major differentiator** — Colorado's health-conscious market responds strongly to non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning products. Multiple top companies lead with this.

5. **Clear service segmentation converts better** — Listing separate pages/sections for residential, deep clean, move-in/out, and commercial (vs. one generic "cleaning" page) significantly increases conversions.

6. **Online booking + instant quotes reduce friction** — Top-converting cleaning websites have quote forms or booking widgets within 1 click of the homepage. Speed-to-response matters.

7. **Social proof is everywhere** — Before/after photos, video testimonials, star ratings displayed prominently. The best sites show Google review count + rating in the hero section.

## Brand Strategy for Lustere Cleaning Co.

### Positioning
**"Denver's Most Trusted Cleaning Service"** — positioning on trust, professionalism, and family values. The name "Lustere" evokes shine, polish, and premium quality.

### Target Audience
- **Primary:** Busy Denver professionals and families (residential)
- **Secondary:** Property managers, landlords, small business owners (commercial/move-in-out)

### Key Differentiators
- Family-owned & locally operated (personal touch vs. corporate chains)
- Eco-friendly products (appeals to Colorado's health-conscious market)
- Background-checked, insured & bonded team
- 100% satisfaction guarantee
- Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)

### Brand Colors
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Deep Blue | `#1E3A8A` | Primary brand, headings, authority |
| Medium Blue | `#2563EB` | CTAs, links, accents |
| Light Blue | `#DBEAFE` | Backgrounds, highlights |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Primary background |
| Grey | `#6B7280` | Body text |
| Light Grey | `#F3F4F6` | Section backgrounds |

### Typography
**Inter** — Clean, modern, highly readable. Professional without being corporate.

## Recommended Next Steps

### Immediate (Week 1)
1. **Replace placeholder info** — Update phone number, email, and website URL with real ones
2. **Deploy the website** — Push `index.html` to Vercel, Netlify, or Cloudflare Pages (free hosting, takes 5 minutes)
3. **Buy the domain** — `lusterecleaning.com` (check availability on Namecheap/Google Domains)
4. **Set up Google My Business** — Create a free GBP listing with business name, hours, service area (Denver Metro), and photos

### Short-Term (Weeks 2-4)
5. **Print materials** — Upload `flyer.html` (print to PDF) and `business-card.html` (print to PDF) to Vistaprint, MOO, or a local Denver print shop
6. **Generate a QR code** — Point it to the website's contact form, replace the placeholder on the flyer
7. **Collect real reviews** — After first 5-10 jobs, ask satisfied clients to leave Google reviews
8. **Set up a real booking form** — Connect the quote form to a service like Formspree, Typeform, or Jobber

### Growth (Month 2+)
9. **Google Ads (Local Services)** — Run "cleaning service Denver" ads ($10-20/day). Google Local Services Ads show the GBP listing with reviews.
10. **Nextdoor & Facebook Groups** — Post in local Denver neighborhood groups. Cleaning services get strong word-of-mouth here.
11. **Before/After Photos** — Start documenting jobs. Add a photo gallery section to the website.
12. **Referral Program** — "$25 off your next clean for every referral" — add to flyer and website
13. **Vehicle wrap** — Once revenue supports it, a branded vehicle wrap is one of the highest-ROI marketing investments for local cleaning companies
