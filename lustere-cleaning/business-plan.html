Marketing Strategy

How to Get Clients — Fast

The fastest path to revenue for a new cleaning business is a combination of Google Business Profile (free), referrals (nearly free), and door-to-door flyers in target neighborhoods. Paid ads come in Month 2–3 once you have reviews.

📍
Phase 1: Google Business Profile (Week 1)

Set up your free Google Business listing immediately. This is the #1 source of local cleaning leads in Denver. Add photos, list all services, and set up "Request a Quote." Aim for 10+ reviews in 30 days.

📄
Phase 1: Flyers & Door Hangers (Week 1–2)

Print 500 flyers and door hangers. Target neighborhoods: Highlands, Cherry Creek, Wash Park, Stapleton, Centennial. Offer a 15% first-clean discount. Distribute yourself — costs under $50.

🤝
Phase 1: Personal Network (Week 1)

Text and message every person you know. Offer a deeply discounted first clean to 3–5 people in exchange for a Google review and referrals. These first reviews are gold.

📱
Phase 2: Nextdoor & Facebook Groups (Week 2–3)

Post in Denver-area Nextdoor and Facebook neighborhood groups. "New local cleaning company, first clean 15% off." These platforms are highly trusted and convert extremely well for home services.

⭐
Phase 2: Review Engine (Month 1–2)

After every job, text clients: "It was a pleasure cleaning your home! If you loved it, a Google review means the world to us: [link]." Even 10 reviews puts you ahead of most competitors.

🎯
Phase 3: Google Local Services Ads (Month 2–3)

Once you have 5+ reviews, run Google Local Services Ads. Budget $15–25/day. These ads show at the very top of Google and are pay-per-lead — only pay when someone contacts you.

90-Day Launch Plan

Your Roadmap to First $5K/Month

1
Days 1–7 — Foundation
Get Legit & Get Visible

Register your business (LLC, ~$50 in CO). Open a business bank account. Get basic liability insurance (~$50/mo). Set up Google Business Profile with photos. Create Instagram/Facebook page. Print flyers and business cards. Tell everyone you know.

2
Days 8–30 — First Clients
Land Your First 8–10 Clients

Distribute 300–500 flyers in target neighborhoods. Post in Nextdoor + Facebook groups 2–3x/week. Offer friends/family discounted cleans for reviews. Book your first recurring clients and lock in bi-weekly schedules. Collect 5+ Google reviews.

3
Days 31–60 — Build Momentum
Double Your Client Base

Launch Google Local Services Ads ($15–20/day). Set up a referral program: "Refer a friend, get $20 off your next clean." Partner with a local real estate agent for move-in/out cleans. Aim for 12–15 recurring clients by end of Month 2.

4
Days 61–90 — Scale
Hit $3,000–$5,000/Month

With 15–20 recurring clients, you're hitting consistent monthly revenue. Consider hiring a part-time cleaner to handle overflow. Start targeting commercial clients (small offices). Optimize your Google Ads based on what's converting. Raise prices slightly for new clients.

Competitive Advantage

Why Lustere Wins

🏠
Family-Owned = Trust

People choose cleaning companies based on trust above all else. A family-owned operation signals personal accountability that franchise chains can't replicate. Lead with your story.

🌿
Eco-Friendly Positioning

Colorado homeowners skew eco-conscious. Using non-toxic, plant-based products is a differentiator that justifies premium pricing and appeals to families with kids and pets.

💬
Communication = Retention

The #1 complaint about cleaning companies is poor communication. Confirm appointments, text when arriving, follow up after. This alone will generate referrals from every client.

⭐
Reviews-First Strategy

10 genuine 5-star Google reviews in your first month puts Lustere above 80% of local competitors who have 0–2 reviews. Reviews are the single highest-ROI activity in Month 1.
