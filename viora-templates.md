## 2. QUALIFICATION SCRIPT (Sales Call / Chat)

**Use this flow during discovery:**

1. **Call volume check**
   "How many inbound calls does your team handle per week?"
   *(Looking for: 10+ calls/week minimum)*

2. **Pain point confirmation**
   "What happens when you miss a call? Do they leave a voicemail or just hang up?"
   *(Looking for: lost revenue, frustrated customers)*

3. **Current solution check**
   "How are you handling calls now? Receptionist? Voicemail? Answering service?"
   *(Looking for: gaps, frustration, manual processes)*

4. **Booking workflow**
   "When someone books with you, do they go into Google Calendar, Calendly, or something else?"
   *(Confirm: Google Calendar compatibility)*

5. **Budget qualification**
   "If I can show you a system that answers every call, qualifies leads, and books appointments automatically for $2,500 setup + monthly service, is that in your budget range?"
   *(Looking for: yes or ability to pay)*

**Green light:** 10+ calls/week + missed calls = pain + Google Calendar + budget fit

## 4. OBJECTION HANDLING

**"That's expensive."**
"I get it. Let me ask — how much is one booked job worth to you? Most of our clients close $500–$5,000 per appointment. If the AI books just one extra client in the first month, it pays for itself. And it's answering every call 24/7 — how much would hiring a full-time receptionist cost?"

**"I need to think about it."**
"Totally fair. What specifically do you need to think through — the setup cost, the monthly service, or how it integrates with your workflow? I can walk you through any of those right now."

**"Can I try it first?"**
"We don't offer free trials because every system is custom-built for your business. But here's what we do: you pay 50% upfront, we build it, you test it live with real calls, and if it's not working the way we promised, we'll refund you before you pay the second half. Sound fair?"

**"I'm already using [answering service/VA]."**
"Got it. How's that working? Are they booking appointments directly into your calendar, or are you still doing manual follow-up? Most services just take messages. Ours qualifies, books, and logs everything automatically. Want to see the difference?"
