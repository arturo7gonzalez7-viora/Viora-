## STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS (LEAD TO CLOSED CLIENT)

### PHASE 1: PROSPECT IDENTIFICATION & RESEARCH
**Jarvis Handles:**

**Step 1: Prospect Scraping**
- Target: 100+ prospects weekly (all businesses except restaurants)
- Sources: LinkedIn Business Premium advanced search
- Filters: Denver metro, 10+ employees, business owner/decision maker
- Industries: Fitness, salons, contractors, professional services, medical, auto, legal

**Step 2: Data Enrichment** 
- Research each prospect: company size, recent posts, growth signals
- Find contact info: email, phone, social profiles
- Identify pain points: hiring, expansion, customer service issues
- Score prospects 1-10 (budget, timing, authority, need)

**Step 3: Database Management**
- Organize in structured files: contact info, research notes, scoring
- Track all interactions and responses
- Flag priority prospects for immediate outreach
- Update prospect status throughout pipeline

### PHASE 2: PERSONALIZED OUTREACH
**Jarvis Handles:**

**Step 4: Message Crafting**
- Write personalized LinkedIn InMails for each prospect
- Customize based on their business type, recent activity, pain points
- Include relevant video testimonial social proof
- Clear value proposition + call to action (15-min call)

**Step 5: Multi-Channel Outreach**
- Primary: LinkedIn InMails (direct messaging)
- Secondary: Email outreach (if email found)
- Tertiary: LinkedIn connection requests with messages
- Timing: Spread out to avoid spam flags

**Step 6: Follow-Up Sequences**
- Day 1: Initial InMail
- Day 4: Follow-up if no response
- Day 8: Final follow-up with case study
- Track all responses and engagement

### PHASE 3: LEAD QUALIFICATION & BOOKING
**Jarvis Handles Initial, Arturo Takes Over:**

**Step 7: Response Monitoring**
- Check LinkedIn, email responses hourly during business hours
- Immediate notification to Arturo for hot leads
- Initial qualification: budget, timeline, decision authority
- Pre-qualify before wasting Arturo's time

**Step 8: Demo Booking**
- Hot leads: Schedule directly with Arturo's calendar
- Warm leads: Additional nurturing sequence
- Cold responses: Polite follow-up and future contact
- Send confirmation and prep materials

**Step 9: Sales Handoff**
- Arturo gets: Full prospect research, conversation history, qualification notes
- Pre-call brief: company background, identified pain points, best approach
- Demo prep: Relevant case studies, pricing recommendations

### PHASE 4: SALES & CLOSING
**Arturo Handles:**

**Step 10: Demo Calls**
- Show AI voice agent capabilities
- Present automation solutions
- Address objections using Jarvis prep materials
- Close for next steps or contract

**Step 11: Proposal & Contract**
- Arturo: Present pricing and terms
- Jarvis: Create proposal documents and contracts
- Follow-up sequence for pending decisions
- Contract execution and payment collection

### PHASE 5: CLIENT DELIVERY
**Jarvis Handles Backend:**

**Step 12: System Building**
- Arturo: Collect client requirements and access
- Jarvis: Build AI voice agents, automations, integrations
- Testing phase with client feedback
- Training and handoff to client

**Step 13: Ongoing Management**
- Monthly performance reports
- System optimization and improvements
- Upselling additional services
- Client satisfaction monitoring
