## 📦 YOUR DAILY WORK (4 Blocks - Complete in Order)

### 🟢 **BLOCK 1: Morning Check (15 minutes)**
**Do this first when you start your day**

✅ Open LinkedIn messages  
✅ Open your email  
✅ Did anyone reply? → Screenshot it → Send to Arturo  
✅ Update Google Sheet: Mark "Reply Received" = Yes  

**Done? Move to Block 2.**

---

### 🟡 **BLOCK 2: Find New Businesses (1 hour)**
**Start this right after Block 1**

**Your goal: Add 20-30 new businesses to Google Sheet**

**Step 1: Find a business on Google Maps**
- Search: "HVAC repair [city name]" or "Roofing company [city name]"
- Pick one with 15+ reviews

**Step 2: Check if it's good quality**
Does it have:
- ✅ Professional website?
- ✅ Phone number visible?
- ✅ 15+ reviews?
- ✅ Located in USA?

If YES to all → Go to Step 3  
If NO to any → Skip it, find another

**Step 3: Find the owner**
- Go to their website → Look for "About" or "Team" page
- Find the owner's name (usually says "Owner" or "CEO")
- Go to LinkedIn → Search "[Owner Name] [Business Name]"
- Click their profile → Look for email in "Contact Info"

**Step 4: Verify the email works**
- Go to emailhippo.com
- Paste the email → Click "Verify"
- Does it say "Valid"? → ✅ Good!
- Does it say "Invalid"? → ❌ Skip this business

**Step 5: Add to Google Sheet**
Fill out these columns:
- Business name
- Website
- Google Maps link
- Phone number
- Owner name
- Owner email (the one you verified!)
- Owner LinkedIn link
- City & State
- Business type (HVAC, Roofing, etc.)
- Review count

**Repeat 20-30 times. Done? Take a break, then move to Block 3.**

---

### 🔵 **BLOCK 3: Send First Messages (45 minutes)**
**Start this 1-2 hours after Block 2**

**Your goal: Contact 30-40 businesses**

**Step 1: Open Google Sheet**
- Find businesses where "First Email Sent" = No
- Pick the oldest 30-40 leads

**Step 2: For each business:**
1. Go to LinkedIn
2. Find the owner's profile
3. Click "Connect" (don't write a message, just connect)
4. Wait 1-2 hours
5. If they accept → Send them this message:

```
Hey [First Name],

Saw you're running [Business Name] in [City]. Quick question — how many calls does your team handle per week?

Most [roofing/HVAC/plumbing] businesses miss 20-30% of calls. We build systems that answer every call 24/7 and book into your calendar.

If you're getting 10+ calls/week, worth a quick chat?
```

**Replace the words in [brackets] with real info:**
- [First Name] = John (their actual name)
- [Business Name] = ABC Roofing (their company)
- [City] = Denver (their city)
- [roofing/HVAC/plumbing] = whatever they do

**Step 3: Mark it done**
- Go back to Google Sheet
- Mark "First Email Sent" = Yes
- Write today's date

**Repeat 30-40 times. Done? Take a break, then move to Block 4.**

---

### 🟣 **BLOCK 4: Send Follow-Ups (45 minutes)**
**Start this 2-3 hours after Block 3**

**Your goal: Follow up with 20-30 people who didn't reply**

**Step 1: Open Google Sheet**
- Find businesses where:
  - "First Email Sent" = Yes
  - "Reply Received" = No
  - You messaged them 3-4 days ago

**Step 2: Send them this:**

```
[First Name], following up — if you're still missing calls and want to see how we fix that, let me know. Happy to show you in 15 minutes.
```

**Step 3: Mark it done**
- Mark "Follow-Up Sent" = Yes in the sheet

**Repeat 20-30 times. Done? Move to final step.**

---

### 📊 **END OF DAY: Send Report (5 minutes)**
**Do this after completing all 4 blocks**

**Copy this and send it to Arturo:**

```
Daily Report — [Today's Date]
✅ Leads added: [how many businesses you added]
✅ First messages sent: [how many new people you contacted]
✅ Follow-ups sent: [how many follow-ups you sent]
✅ Replies received: [how many people replied]
✅ Interested leads: [how many want to talk]
```

**Send this EVERY DAY when you finish. Never skip it.**

## 🔧 HOW TO VERIFY EMAILS (So They Don't Bounce)

### Option 1: Find Email on LinkedIn (BEST)
1. Go to their LinkedIn profile
2. Click "Contact Info"
3. Is there an email? → Use it!
4. Write "LinkedIn" in the "Email Source" column

### Option 2: Use Hunter.io (SECOND BEST)
1. Go to hunter.io
2. Type: Business website + Owner name
3. Copy the verified email
4. Write "Hunter" in the "Email Source" column

### Option 3: Guess and Verify (LAST OPTION)
**Common email patterns:**
- john@abcroofing.com
- johnsmith@abcroofing.com
- jsmith@abcroofing.com

**Then verify it:**
1. Go to emailhippo.com
2. Paste the email
3. Click "Verify"
4. Only use it if it says "Valid"

### Option 4: Call and Ask
1. Call the business
2. Say: "Hi, I'm trying to reach [Owner Name] by email. What's their email address?"
3. Write it down

**⚠️ IMPORTANT: If you can't find a valid email, DON'T add the business.**
