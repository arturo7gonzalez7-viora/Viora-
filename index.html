Frequently Asked Questions

Which calendar systems does Viora integrate with? +

Viora integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, Calendly, Acuity Scheduling, and most popular scheduling platforms. During setup, we'll connect directly to your existing calendar so appointments sync in real-time.

What happens if the AI can't handle a call? +

If the AI encounters a question outside its scope or a complex scenario, it smoothly transfers the call to you or your team. You can also configure the AI to take a detailed message and notify you immediately for follow-up.

How long does setup take? +

Most businesses are fully operational within 7-10 business days. This includes AI training on your services, calendar integration, workflow customization, and testing to ensure everything runs smoothly before going live.

Does Viora filter spam and robocalls? +

Yes. Viora is trained to identify and filter spam, robocalls, and irrelevant inquiries. You'll only receive notifications and appointments from legitimate customers interested in your services.

Can the AI transfer calls to a human? +

Absolutely. You can configure when and how the AI transfers calls to you or your team. Whether it's for complex questions, VIP customers, or specific scenarios, we ensure a smooth handoff every time.

Is my customer data secure? +

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption for all calls and data storage. Viora is built with security and privacy as top priorities, and we offer HIPAA-compliant options for healthcare providers.
