## 💰 BULLETPROOF RISK MANAGEMENT

### Position Sizing Formula
- **Account Size × 1% Risk ÷ Stop Loss Distance = Contracts**
- **$10,000 account, 20pt NQ stop = $100 ÷ ($5×20) = 1 contract**
- **Under $5,000 = Use MES/MNQ (micro contracts)**

### Non-Negotiable Rules
- ✅ Maximum 1% account risk per trade
- ✅ 2:1 minimum reward-to-risk ratio  
- ✅ Maximum 3 trades per day
- ✅ Stop after 2 consecutive losses
- ✅ Daily loss limit: 3% of account
- ✅ Set stops BEFORE entering - never move against you

### Stop Loss Guidelines
- **NQ:** 10-25 points ($50-125 per contract)
- **ES:** 3-8 points ($75-200 per contract) 
- **MNQ/MES:** Proportional (1/4 size)

## 🧠 TRADING PSYCHOLOGY

### Beginner Mistake Prevention
- 🚫 **Revenge Trading:** Max daily losses, walk away
- 🚫 **Overtrading:** Max 3 trades/day, quality > quantity
- 🚫 **Moving Stops:** Honor stops, never move against you  
- 🚫 **FOMO Trading:** Wait for setups, don't chase
- 🚫 **Emotional Sizing:** Use math formulas, not feelings
