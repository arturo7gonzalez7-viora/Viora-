
## ARV Pro Calculator Research (for Steven CRM)

Key formulas:
- ARV = Sale Price + Repairs
- MAO = (ARV × rule%) − Rehab (rule% slider 60-80%, default 70%)
- Gross Profit = ARV − Purchase − Rehab
- Net Profit = ARV − Purchase − Rehab − Holding − Selling − Contingency
- ROI% = Net Profit ÷ Total Invested
